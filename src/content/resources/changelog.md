## Recent Changes

### April 2026 — v3.0: Multi-page Field Guide
Migrated from single-page HTML to Astro-based multi-page architecture. Content organized into hybrid matrix: Foundations, Tasks, Settings, Domains, and Resources. Markdown-based authoring for easier maintenance and community contributions.

### April 2026 — GEO optimization & new content sections
Added PHI Safety Check interactive tool, Model Quick Reference, Before & After documentation examples, Prompt Patterns guide, and IEP Goal Clarity Toolkit. Plus structured data, llms.txt, and updated robots.txt.

### March 2026 — v2.0: Harmonic Systems rebrand
Full rebrand under Harmonic Systems. Added origin story, project showcase, updated navigation, and design polish.

### March 2026 — Digital Media Literacy section
Timeline of how media has shifted, why it matters for SLPs, and where clinical communication expertise intersects with the evolving digital landscape.

### March 2026 — AI chat assistant, evidence brief, prompt templates
Claude-powered chatbot with PHI detection, cross-disciplinary research summary, and copyable prompt templates.

### May 2025 — Original launch
SLP/IO launched as a portfolio of speech-language pathology coding projects.
